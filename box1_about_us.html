Who is BuXpay?

The BuXpay wealth management platform launched in 2013 with the goal of becoming the leading independent wealth platform for the international financial services market. Since then we’ve attracted Financial Advisers and their investors from all over the world to hold their investments with us.

Manx heritage

We’re proud of our Isle of Man heritage and this even extends to our name – “Ardan” is Manx Gaelic for “platform”. Our Island location gives us many benefits and our head office is in Douglas, capital of the Isle of Man.

Independent recognition

Our strong progress has been independently recognised with a number of industry awards: International Investment Awards - Best International Platform 2016, 2017, 2018, 2020, 2021, and 2022. International Adviser – Product and Service Awards 2017 – International Platform of the year.

Strong owner

In 2016 we became part of International Financial Group Limited (IFGL). Other members of IFGL include RL360, RL360° Services and Friends Provident International.
